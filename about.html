About Velvet Vogue

Founded by John Finlo, Velvet Vogue was born out of a passion for creativity and fashion-forward thinking. Since opening our doors, we have redefined modern style for young adults by blending casualwear with elegance.

Whether you’re dressing up for a night out or keeping it cool and casual, our clothing and accessories help you express who you truly are.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to express their true selves through fashion. We strive to provide high-quality, trendy, and affordable styles with a seamless shopping experience.

Our Vision

To be the go-to brand for fashion-conscious youth around the globe and a trendsetter in sustainable and expressive fashion.
